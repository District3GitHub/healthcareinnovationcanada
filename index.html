Known for its thriving AI centers, Canada is also home to some of the most promising healthtech.

Healthcare Innovation from Canada (HIC) is powered by three Canadian health technology accelerators: CTS (Health Technology Catalyst), District 3 and MaRS Discovery District, with over 300+ health technology startups. The HIC consortium helps startups grow their business, raise capital and generate customer connections. Connect with us to meet top Canadian startups in digital health and MedTech!

Meet the accelerators

Health Technology Catalyst

Specialized in innovative healthcare technology and digital health solutions, CTS is an à la carte growth accelerator who responds to the particular challenges and needs of each startup. With CTS behind you, you’ll get strategic coaching to secure financing, effective commercialization tools that will help you succeed across Canada and internationally, and an introduction to our dynamic ecosystem of key medical and business players.

District 3

District3 nurtures and develops multidisciplinary startup teams that harness emerging tech to create businesses with global impact. We look for the brightest of minds and we offer the most rigorous training. We bridge the knowledge, skills and resource gap between building a product and building a company.

MaRS

MaRS brings together the innovation community to grow the economy and make an impact. From advisory services to connections to talent, capital, customers and more, MaRS offers a range of high-value services that help high-growth companies succeed. We also provide access to the MaRS ecosystem, a curated community of entrepreneurs, investors, corporates, academics and government partners.
